Overview of HELP field XMM-13hr

This HELP field is defined by SPIRE coverage of the XMM-Newton, also known as the High Throughput X-ray Spectroscopy Mission and the X-ray Multi-Mirror Mission 13 hr (XMM-13hr) field.
