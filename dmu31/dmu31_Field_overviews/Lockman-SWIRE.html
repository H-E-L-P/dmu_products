Overview of HELP field Lockman-SWIRE

This HELP field is defined by SPIRE coverage of the Lockman hole Spitzer Wide InfraRed Extragalactic (SWIRE) survey field..

Full information on each of these surveys is available at those links. Click here to get a simple VOX query to get you started.
