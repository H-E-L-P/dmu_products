Overview of HELP field ELAIS-N1

This HELP field is defined by SPIRE coverage of the European Large Area Infrared Survey field North 1 (ELAIS-N1).

Full information on each of these surveys is available at those links. Click here to get a simple VOX query to get you started.

Key stats:
Area 13.5 square degrees
Masterlist objects 4 026 292

Photo-z objects 2 714 686 (x %)
CIGALE objects 49 985 (x %)
